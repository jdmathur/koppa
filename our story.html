Our journey so far

All of this started with nothing but passion for Indian coffee!

Our passion lies in discovering what makes Indian coffee truly Indian – whether it’s sourcing from the oldest plantations to elevating our age old secrets of Chicory. Koppa brings you the quintessential South Indian brew available to every coffee enthusiast across the globe, and offering the best blend of flavours in every single sip.

Origins

We source our beans from some of the oldest and most established coffee estates, from the birthplace of Indian Coffee – Chikmagalur. Fabled to be the place where the first coffee bean was planted in Indian soil by Baba Budan, we are proud to say that the coffee beans we use have a rich heritage that can be traced all the way back to the 17th century.

Roasting

We develop the quintessential flavour profiles of South Indian coffee with an elaborate roast profiling process, developing hundreds of unique aroma compounds that come together to give you that unforgettable, authentic brew. Our coffee beans and chicory roots undergo this process by hand with love, care and painstaking attention-to-detail.

Degassing

When green coffee beans are roasted, a number of gases form inside them, resulting in small bubbles that detract from its flavour and aroma, leading to uneven extractions when brewed with water. Therefore, the ‘degassing’ process is all about giving the roasted coffee beans time to release their gases, while still retaining their crisp freshness and flavour profile.

Grinding and Bleanding

The coffee beans are ground at our state-of-the-art facility before the blending process is begun. We use the finest grinding and blending machinery to ensure that the purity and integrity of our ingredients is fully retained, negating the need for any preservatives, additives or other artificial ingredients.

Finishing up

This unique base concentrate combines our ground coffee beans with powdered chicory root. The mixture is then tamped into layers and drip-brewed carefully with hot water to create our signature Coffee Concentrate. Our advanced packaging facility ensures that our coffee stays fresh and vibrantly flavourful long after it reaches your shelves.
